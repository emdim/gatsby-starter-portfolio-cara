## About me

I studied Computer Engineering and Informatics at the University of Patras.  
During my studies, I learned to create video games and became Co-Founder of [eNVy softworks](https://envysoftworks.com) a company that creates video games and VR applications. Also, I was a member of the IEEE Student Branch of the University of Patras and had the Chairman's position the years 2016 - 2017.  
In my free time, I like to go to the gym, playing video games and 3D printing stuff.
